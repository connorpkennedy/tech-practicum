About the Dream Team

The Dream team is a group of incredible people that have discovered their gifts and passions and are actively serving in them. From creating a comfortable environment by serving on a Host Team, to leading the next generation to Jesus by serving on a Student Ministries Team, to creating a dynamic experience with the Service Experience Team, to tearing down barriers across our city by leading a small group or partnering with the Dream Center Team - we are a group that forcefully advances the Kingdom of God.

OUR MISSION

The mission of the Dream Team is to build the body of Chirst by each person fulfilling their calling and serving to make an eternal difference.
